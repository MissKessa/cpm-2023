Game description

The board

In the board there is 1 leader ghost (the one in the first row) and 5 other different types of ghosts.

There is a total of 5 different squad, a squad is a group of 3 ghosts of the same type.

In the last row of the board, there are the ghost hunters, and they will be the ones under your control.


The objective

The user will try to prevent the definitive takeover of the castle by the ghosts by eliminating their leader and preventing a complete squad of 3 ghosts of the same type from keeping on the board.


Game rules

The player will have a maximum of 7 dice rolls in each game

This will have two possible values: 1 and 2


After each roll, the player will move any of the ghostbuster one or two positions (depending on the value of the die) by clicking them in vertical movement

The ghost that occupies the square that the ghostbuster reaches will be considered eliminated and will disappear from the board


Game over

The game ends when the 7 possible moves are used. At the end of the game:

  • If the leader and at least one ghost from each crew have been eliminated, the discount obtained will be 25%.
  • If the leader of the ghosts survives but at least one ghost has been eliminated from each group, the discount will be 10%
  • The email of the user
  • In other cases, the user will not get a discount.
