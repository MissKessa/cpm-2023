Reserving

You will see:



  • Summary of all the castle information with:
    • Name of the castle
    • Country of the castle
    • Enchantments of the castle
    • Room price of the castle
  • Form to fill to make the reservation:
    • Id
    • Number of people
    • Number of rooms
    • Number of days
    • Starting date

For making the reservation, you must mark the checkbox checking that you are 18 or older.

If you don't mark it this window will be shown:



You can click:

  • Ok: You will be back to the reservation window to mark the checkbox
  • Cancel: You will go back to the castle window

Filling the form

The id and claiming discounts

When an id is written, it's checked if it has a discount associated.

If it has a discount, the Claim discount button will be enabled to use the discount if the user wants

When the discount is used, the final price will be recalculated


The number of people and rooms

You can chose the number of people you want, and it won't affect the final price

The number of rooms will be limited by the number of people. There cannot be more than 2 people in a room, or more rooms than people


The number of days and the starting date

You can chose any number of days, but the starting date has to be at least the current date


Ending the reservation

You can:

  • Formalize: When the id is filled, and you will go to the formalization warning window
  • Back: You will go back to the castle window, and the information of the form filled will be keep
