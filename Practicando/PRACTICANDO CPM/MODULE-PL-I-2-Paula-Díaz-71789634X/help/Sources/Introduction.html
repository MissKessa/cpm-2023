Castlevania

Welcome to the Castlevania app where you can:

  • Make a reservation
  • Play a game and gain discounts (you don't need to make a reservation)
  • Or both.


In this window you can:

  • Play the game by clicking the Game button
  • Filter the available castles
  • Changing the language
  • View the details of the castles and make a reservation

Options in the menu bar

  • Reservation
    • New: Initializes the app again
    • Gain discount: You can go directly to the game but you will lose the reservation
    • Exit: Exits the whole app
  • Help
    • Help contents: Shows the help support
    • About: Shows the author of the app

Making a reservation

To make a reservation you need:

  1. Click on the View button of the castle you want.
  2. You will see all the detailed information of the castle
  3. You will fill all the information of the reservation
  4. You will see the warning before formalizing
  5. You will see the formalization window
  6. A done window will be shown to just click to the finish button, and initialize the whole app
