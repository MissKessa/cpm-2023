The different kinds of filters

You can select what kind of castles do you wan to see by filtering by:

  • Country
  • Enchantments
  • Price

You can also remove all the filters.


Filtering by country

To filter by country just click on the combo box and chose the country you want.

Only the castles with that country will be shown.


Filtering by enchantments

To filter by enchantments, click on the button Enchantments.

This will be the window shown:


Here you will see all the possible enchantments to filter:

  1. The enchantments marked are the ones that will be searched in the list of castles.
    • If you click on Select all filters, all the enchanments will be marked.
    • If you click on Unselect all filters, all the enchanments will be unmarked.
  2. If you click Accept, the filter chosen will be applied.
  3. If you click Cancel, the filter chosen won't be applied. The filter applied will be the one before.

Filtering by price

To filter by price just click on the combo box and chose the range of prices you want.

Only the castles in that range will be shown.
